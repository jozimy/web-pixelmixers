Last updated: January 2026

PixelMixers s.r.o. ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data. Data Controller

PixelMixers s.r.o., Bratislava, Slovakia. For data-related inquiries, contact us at hello@pixelmixers.com.

What We Collect

We may collect the following personal data: name, email address, and message content submitted through our contact form; technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information collected automatically via cookies and server logs; and usage data including pages visited, time spent, and referral source.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on: your consent (e.g., when you submit our contact form); legitimate interests (e.g., improving our website and services); and contractual necessity (e.g., when we need your data to deliver a project).

How We Use Your Data

To respond to your enquiries and provide our services; to improve our website, content, and user experience; and to comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, or as required by law. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 2 years unless you request earlier deletion.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to: access, correct, or delete your personal data; restrict or object to processing; data portability; and withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@pixelmixers.com.
