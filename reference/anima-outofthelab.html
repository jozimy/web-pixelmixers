THE PRIZE

What you win

€50k for the winner

→ €15k in non-dilutive funding

→ A €35k six-month operational support delivered by our team of seasoned entrepreneurs, we’ll work with you to: validate the market and secure early client, turn research into an investable business model, build a solid non-scientific deck, access non-dilutive funding opportunities.

→ Access to our network of top-tier investors, corporates, and mentors

→ Workspace at our offices in the heart of Paris, at the Climate House

→ A 1-year licence to Asteria to accelerate your R&D

TOP 5 receives

→ Visibility across our communication channels

→ Warm introductions to the right people in our ecosystem (corporate partners, operators, and experts)

→ Fast-track access to our Programs (Incubation, Studio, Investment)

All Top 5 founders are welcome to work from our Climate House offices!

Timeline

→ Applications open: March 10th to April 30th 2026

→ Top 5 announced: June 2026

→ Winners revealed: July 2026

 

 

Who should apply?

→ TRL 3 to 5: Projects with validated concepts or early-stage prototypes

→ Bio-inspired approaches (function, form, or process)

→ Researchers, project leads or early-stage startups ready to transition from concept to market.

→ Open to applicants from any country

→ Ambition to build something that scales
