(02) About

A small studio with a serious obsession for craft

PixelMixers is a Bratislava-based design studio building digital products for brands that care about how things look, feel, and work. We are a tight crew of designers, developers, and strategists who believe the web deserves better than templates and trend-chasing. Every project starts with listening, moves through deliberate design, and ships as something we are genuinely proud of. We work with startups finding their voice and established companies ready to evolve theirs.
