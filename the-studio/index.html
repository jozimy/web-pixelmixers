A small studio with
a serious obsession.

We are PixelMixers — a tight-knit team of designers, developers, and digital thinkers based in Bratislava, building websites for clients across Europe and beyond. Founded in 2018, we've stayed deliberately small because we believe the best creative work happens when the people doing it care deeply.

Est. 2018 Based in Bratislava, SK Working globally
002/

How we got here

PixelMixers started in 2018 in a small shared office in Bratislava. Marek and Filip — one a designer, the other a strategist — had been freelancing separately for years and kept running into the same problem: clients deserved better than what most agencies were delivering. Too much process, too little craft.

So they teamed up. Lucia joined six months later, bringing the engineering discipline the studio needed. Zoe came aboard in 2021, adding motion and interaction design to the mix. Today we're four people — and we intend to stay that way. Small enough to care about every pixel, experienced enough to deliver work that competes with studios ten times our size.

We've shipped over 50 projects across four continents. We've turned down projects that didn't feel right and doubled down on the ones that did. Every site we build is a reflection of who we are: meticulous, curious, and a little obsessive about the details.

003/

The way we work

01

Start with feeling

Before wireframes, before sitemaps, we ask: what should it feel like to visit this site? That emotional target guides every decision that follows.

02

Design in context

We design in the browser as early as possible. Static mockups lie — real screens on real devices tell the truth about spacing, motion, and readability.

03

Words first

Copy isn't filler. We write (or rewrite) the words before we design around them, because layout should serve the message, not the other way around.

04

Performance is a feature

A beautiful site that loads in five seconds is a failed site. We obsess over Core Web Vitals, image optimization, and lean code from day one.

05

Accessibility always

Semantic HTML, proper ARIA, keyboard navigation, sufficient contrast — accessibility isn't an afterthought, it's baked into our process from the start.

06

Honest handoffs

When we deliver, you get clean, documented code and a clear guide to maintaining it. No vendor lock-in, no mystery. Your site is yours.
